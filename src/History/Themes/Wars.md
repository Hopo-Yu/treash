# History of Wars

Wars have shaped the course of human history significantly. This history encompasses conflicts from the ancient times like the Trojan War, to World Wars in the 20th century, and numerous other conflicts around the globe. Examining the history of wars gives us a deep insight into the changing geopolitics, technologies, and philosophies over the centuries.
