# History of Revolutions

Revolutions have been the pivot points in history, steering humanity towards new directions. From the philosophical revolutions in ancient Greece to the more recent technological revolutions, each phase brought forth a wave of change. The timeline of revolutions is a testimony to the evolving human thought and the incessant drive for a better, more equitable society.
